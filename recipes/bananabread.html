Banana Bread

Almost no fat banana bread

I know what you're thinking, banana bread with no fat? Ain't no way! I'm in disbelief too. And even after trying this bread out for myself, I think they're lying to us. My tastebuds enjoy the flavor of this bread too much for it to be fat-free.

The moistness of this bread will remind you of Niagara Falls. So much so that you should probably wear a raincoat while eating it. Or at least one of those bibs they give you when you go to Red Lobster.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup banana, mashed
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 2 egg whites

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8x4-inch loaf pan.
  2. Stir flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon together in a large bowl. Add banana, applesauce, and egg whites; stir just until combined. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 50 to 55 minutes. Turn out onto a wire rack and allow to cool before slicing.
